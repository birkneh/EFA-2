Welcome to Evidence for Action (EFA)

Evidence for Action (EFA) is a pioneering Non-Governmental Research Organization formed by a diverse group of experts from around the globe. Our mission is to support the generation and efficient utilization of evidence, particularly in resource-constrained settings. We believe that robust, actionable evidence is critical to driving impactful decision-making and improving public health outcomes.

At EFA, we collaborate with a wide array of stakeholders, including research organizations, funders, and researchers worldwide. Our work is centered on the following key areas:

  • Cutting-Edge Research: We conduct innovative research that pushes the boundaries of knowledge and provides essential insights into pressing global health issues.
  • Preventive, Therapeutic, and Diagnostic Innovation: EFA is at the forefront of developing and evaluating new approaches to disease prevention, treatment, and diagnosis, ensuring that these innovations are effective and accessible.
  • Research Consultancy: We offer expert consultancy services in study design, data analysis, and reporting, helping researchers and organizations produce high-quality, credible findings that can influence policy and practice.
  • Capacity Strengthening: Recognizing the importance of building local capacity, EFA provides training and mentorship programs aimed at empowering researchers and healthcare professionals in resource-limited settings.

Through our work, EFA strives to make a meaningful difference in the world by ensuring that evidence-based approaches are at the heart of global health initiatives.

About Us

Evidence for Action (EFA) was established in 2024 with the mission to catalyze the generation and utilization of evidence in low-resource settings. Recognizing the critical need for high-quality, actionable data in these environments, EFA brings together a global network of experts dedicated to advancing public health and healthcare delivery where it's needed most.

Core Values and Vision

  • Collaboration: We believe that the most effective solutions emerge from strong partnerships. We work closely with local and international stakeholders to co-create strategies that address the unique challenges of low-resource settings.
  • Efficient Use of Evidence: Our approach is driven by the principle that evidence should not only be generated but also be efficiently utilized to inform decision-making. We strive to ensure that research findings are translated into actionable policies and practices that make a tangible difference.
  • Policy Advocacy: We are committed to advocating for evidence-based policies that prioritize the health and well-being of communities in low-resource settings. Through strategic engagement with policymakers and stakeholders, we work to ensure that the voices of those most affected by health inequities are heard and addressed.

EFA envisions a world where evidence-driven approaches lead to sustainable health improvements and where even the most resource-constrained communities have access to the tools and knowledge needed to thrive.

Our Services

At Evidence for Action (EFA), we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to support the advancement of public health through research and innovation, particularly in low- and middle-income countries (LMICs). Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients and partners, ensuring that evidence is effectively translated into practice and policy. Our key service areas include:

Translation Research

  • Implementation Research: We specialize in conducting implementation research that bridges the gap between research findings and real-world practice. Our work focuses on understanding how interventions can be adapted and scaled in diverse contexts to achieve the greatest impact.
  • Policy Advocacy: EFA is committed to ensuring that evidence informs policy decisions. We work closely with policymakers, providing them with the research insights needed to create policies that improve health outcomes in resource-constrained settings.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: We believe that successful health interventions require the active involvement of all stakeholders. EFA facilitates meaningful engagement with communities, healthcare providers, policymakers, and other key stakeholders to ensure that their perspectives and needs are incorporated into research and policy initiatives.

Research Consultancy

  • Design and Implementation: Our team provides expert consultancy on the design and implementation of health research projects in LMICs. We work with organizations to develop robust study designs that are contextually relevant and methodologically sound.
  • Analysis and Reporting: EFA offers comprehensive support in the analysis and reporting of research findings. Our team ensures that data is accurately interpreted and effectively communicated to relevant audiences, maximizing the impact of the research.

Training and Capacity Strengthening

EFA is dedicated to building the capacity of researchers, healthcare professionals, and organizations in LMICs. We offer a range of training programs and workshops that equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to conduct high-quality research and implement effective health interventions. Our capacity-strengthening initiatives are designed to foster local expertise and leadership in public health.

Innovation in Therapeutic, Preventive, and Diagnostic Spaces

At the forefront of health innovation, EFA is actively involved in the development and evaluation of new therapeutic, preventive, and diagnostic solutions. We work to identify and promote innovative approaches that have the potential to transform health outcomes in resource-limited settings. Our focus is on ensuring that these innovations are accessible, affordable, and adaptable to the unique challenges faced by LMICs.

Through these services, EFA strives to contribute to the global effort to improve health and well-being in underserved populations. Our commitment to excellence, collaboration, and innovation ensures that our clients and partners can achieve their goals and make a lasting impact.
